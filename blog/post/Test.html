    What is Open Savannah?

    Open Savannah is a new volunteer civic tech organization. We are part of the Code for America Brigade network, and are made up of developers, designers, data geeks, citizen activists, and many others who use creative technology to solve civic and social problems.

    What We Do

    Our ultimate goal is to make government work for the people, by the people, in the 21st Century. This means ensuring that municipal governments have the resources to keep up with the rapidly changing demands of residents and businesses.
